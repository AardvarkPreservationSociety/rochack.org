Hacker Night

A place for people to build and learn about technology
Every Monday night at 8pm in Hylan 301

Let's do awesome things

Hacker Nights are here to help you build whatever satisfies your curiosity. In the past, we've built things like UR Bus Schedules, the Cluster Graph, and crazy IRC bots.

Or anything at all

You don't need a crazy idea or project to join in. Come bring homework, programming questions, whatever. In addition to building cool things, these nights are all about building a community, and we'd love for as many people as possible to be a part of it.

Not just for programmers

All you need is the desire to build. If you don't know how to code, solder a circuit, or setup a server we'll find someone who can teach you.

Try it out

If any of this sounds good to you, come join the fun every Monday night at 8pm in Hylan 301.
