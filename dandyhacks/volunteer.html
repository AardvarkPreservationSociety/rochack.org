Want to volunteer ?

What is it?

It is a great opportunity for you to make a positive impact on your community and strengthen your leadership skills! We have no requirements besides having a strong commitment. Our hackathon committee loves what they do and we hope you will too!

What would I do?

There are a lot of open opportunities including contacting sponsors, advertising and helping on site!

Can I be both a volunteer and a participant at the event?

Unfortunately not. There are perks to both so you decide what you would be interested in doing more!
